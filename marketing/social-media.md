## Content Calendar Framework

### Weekly Themes

| Day | Theme | Platform Focus | Content Type |
|-----|-------|---------------|--------------|
| **Monday** | Industry Insights | LinkedIn | Data, trends, statistics posts |
| **Tuesday** | Product Education | Twitter/X | How-it-works threads, feature highlights |
| **Wednesday** | Customer Stories | LinkedIn | Case studies, testimonials, before/after stories |
| **Thursday** | Community Engagement | Twitter/X | Questions, polls, discussion starters |
| **Friday** | Thought Leadership | LinkedIn | Opinion pieces, future-of-PM content |

### Monthly Content Pillars

**Week 1: Pain Points**
- Highlight a specific property management challenge
- Share data on the cost/impact of that challenge
- Tease the solution without being salesy

**Week 2: Solutions & Education**
- Explain how AI/technology addresses the Week 1 pain point
- Share how-to content, tips, and frameworks
- Product demos or feature highlights

**Week 3: Social Proof**
- Customer stories and case studies
- Before/after metrics
- User testimonials and quotes

**Week 4: Industry & Vision**
- Broader industry trends and analysis
- Future of property management
- Company mission and values content

### Posting Frequency

| Platform | Posts/Week | Best Times (ET) |
|----------|-----------|-----------------|
| LinkedIn | 3-4 | Tue-Thu, 8:00-9:00 AM or 12:00-1:00 PM |
| Twitter/X | 5-7 | Mon-Fri, 8:00 AM, 12:00 PM, or 5:00 PM |

### Engagement Rules

1. **Respond to every comment** within 4 hours during business hours
2. **Comment on 5-10 relevant posts daily** from property management professionals, industry publications, and complementary SaaS companies
3. **Repost/share** customer content and industry news with added commentary
4. **Never argue** with negative comments -- acknowledge, offer to help, take it to DMs
5. **Tag people and companies** when referencing their work or ideas (builds reciprocal engagement)
6. **Use 3-5 hashtags on LinkedIn**, 1-2 on Twitter/X. Core tags: #PropertyManagement #PropTech #AI

### KPIs to Track

- Impressions per post
- Engagement rate (likes + comments + shares / impressions)
- Profile visits from posts
- Website clicks from social
- DMs received from content
- Follower growth rate (weekly)
- Conversion: social lead to demo booked
