## Email Sending Best Practices

- **Send Time:** Tuesday-Thursday, 8:00-9:30 AM in the recipient's local timezone
- **Follow-up Cadence:** Day 1 (send), Day 3 (bump), Day 7 (new angle), Day 14 (breakup email)
- **Personalization:** Always research the company's portfolio size and market before sending. Reference specific pain points if visible on their website or reviews.
- **Subject Line Testing:** A/B test subject lines in batches of 50. Track open rates before scaling.
- **CAN-SPAM Compliance:** Include physical mailing address, unsubscribe option, and accurate sender information in all outbound emails.
